About Meridian Hotel & Spa

A family-run boutique hotel on the banks of Lake Pichola, built around quiet comfort and genuine hospitality.

Twenty Years by the Lake

Meridian Hotel & Spa opened its doors in 2006 in a restored haveli overlooking Lake Pichola. What began as a six-room guesthouse has grown into a 32-room boutique property, while keeping the same handwritten welcome and home-style hospitality our first guests remember.

Every room is designed around natural light and lake views, and our kitchen blends Rajasthani thalis with familiar international favourites. Whether you're here for a wedding, a business stay or a weekend escape, our team plans the details so you don't have to.
